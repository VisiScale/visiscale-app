Send by Text Send by Email

You can request reviews any way you want — text, email, QR code — but hands down the best results come from doing it in person. Here's how.

Right before collecting payment, offer a small discount in exchange for a review. Pull up the QR code and coach them on what to say while they're writing it. Technically against Google's ToS, but pretty much impossible to track since it's happening in person.

Coach them to include:

  • The service they had done — "lawn mowing," "panel upgrade," etc.
  • Their neighborhood or town — "in Essex, VT," "in Williston," etc.
  • Your name or business name — "my technician Jack was amazing" or "VisiScale is who you call for this" both work. Google uses these to associate your name and brand with your service category.
